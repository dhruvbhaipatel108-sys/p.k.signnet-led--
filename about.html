Company history

From practical LED work to complete lighting solutions.

P.K. Signnate LED began with practical LED and signage projects and grew into a full-service lighting partner for residential and commercial spaces. Today, the team handles design guidance, product selection, lighting layout, installation, and maintenance support.

Every project is approached with attention to brightness, durability, wiring safety, clean mounting, and the final mood of the space.
